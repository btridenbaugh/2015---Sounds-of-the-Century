Diplo
Diplo is a GRammy-nominated American DJ, music producer, rapper, and songwriter from California. He founded and manages the record company Mad Decent, as well as co-founding the non-profit orginization Heaps Decent. He was also the co-creator of the electronic dancehall music project, Major Lazer. Also his debut album Revolution EP in 2013 reached number 68 on the Us Billboard 200. He was previously apart of the British group M.I.A. as well, but shifted to the Jamaican project, Major Lazer. He has also in recent years collaborated with many popular artists, such as Usher, Justin Beiber, and Skrillex.
