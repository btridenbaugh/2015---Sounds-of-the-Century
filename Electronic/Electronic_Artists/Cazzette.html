Cazzette
Cazzette is a Swedish electronic music duo founded in 2011 by musicians Alexander Bjorkulnd and Sebastian Furrer.  The duo is best known for their song "Beam Me Up" from their debut album Eject in 2012. They are also known for their song "Sleepless" that was made in 2014, and charted in Sweden, making it a cerified gold song. The have also performed with the Swedish DJ Avicii at a show in New York in 2012, and again in Manchester in 2014. 
They can often be found with a mask shaped as a cassette as well.
