Calvin Harris
Calvin Harris is a Scottish singer, songwriter, record prodcer, and DJ. He created his debut album I Created Disco in 2007, which later became a gold-selling album. He later created three more albums, the more notable ones being 18 Months, which was made in 2012, and the album Motion, which was created in 2014. The album 18 Months holds the record in UK for the most top ten songs from one studio album on the UK singles chart, even surpassing Michael Jackson.
