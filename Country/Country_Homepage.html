COUNTRY

 Country music is a genre of American popular music that originated in Southern United States, in Atlanta, Georgia in the 1920's. It takes roots from American folk music and Western music. It often consist of ballads and dance tunes with simple forms and harmonies with string instruments such as banjos, electic and acoustic guitars, fiddles, and harmonicas.
