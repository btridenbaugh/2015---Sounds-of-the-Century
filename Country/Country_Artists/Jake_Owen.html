Jake Owen
Jake Owen is an American country music artist. He is signed to RCA Nashville in 2006, he released his debut album Startin' with Me that year. This album produced three singles, all of which reached Top 20 on the Billboard Hot Country Songs chart. His debut single "Yee Haw", "Startin' with Me", and "Something About a Woman". A second album, 2009's Easy Does It, has accounted fro three more singles: "Don't Think I Can't Love You", "Eight Second Ride", and "Tell Me". In September 2011, Owen achieved his first number-one hit on the country charts with the title track to his third album Barefoot Blue Jean Night, as did with "Alone with You", "The One That Got Away", and "Anywhere with You". His fourth album Days of Gold produced two singles with its title track, which broke Top 20 in 2014, and number one single "Beachin'".
