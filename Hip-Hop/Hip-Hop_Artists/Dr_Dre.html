Dr. Dre
Dr.  Dre is an American record producer, rapper, and entrepreneur. He is the founder and current CEO of Aftermath Entertainment and Beats Electronic. He was also a previous co-owner and artist of Death Row Records. As an artist he has produced albums, and he has overseen the careers of many rappers, including Snoop Dogg, Eminem, Xzibit, 50 Cent, The Game, and Kendrick Lamar. He is also credited as a key figure in West Coast G-funk.
