Lil Wayne
Lil Wayne is an American rapper, songwriter, and entrpreneur from New Orleans, Louisiana. In 1991, Lil Wayne joined Cash Money Records, at the age of nine. He was the youngest member of the label, and half of the duo The B.G.'z with Lil' Doogie. He later in 1996, Lil Wayne formed the Southern hip-hop group Hot Boys. The Hot Boys debuted with Get It How U Live! that same year. Lil Wayne gained the most success with the album Guerilla Warfare. He is also the flagship artist of Cash Money Records, and is the CEO of his own inprint, Yound Money Entertainment.
