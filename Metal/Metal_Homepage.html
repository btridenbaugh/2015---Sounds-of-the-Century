METAL

 Metal, also referred to as Heavy Metal, is a genre of rock music that developed in the late 1960's. With roots of blues rock and psychedelic rock, the bands developed a thick, massive sound, characterized by highly amplified distortion, guitar solos, emphatic beats, and loudness. Heavy metal lyrics are also associated with masculinity, aggression, and machismo.
